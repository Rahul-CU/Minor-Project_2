Purpose of this App

Vehicle number plate detection is essential for law enforcement to identify and track vehicles involved in crimes. It aids in enforcing traffic regulations and managing parking facilities efficiently.

How to use the App?

Using our vehicle number plate detection web app is straightforward. Begin by uploading your vehicle image by clickibg the upload button to initiate the process. The detected number plate will then be displayed on the screen.
